About Sensory Steps

Sensory Steps is designed to revolutionize the lives of visually impaired individuals by providing an AI-driven wearable that enhances mobility and independence. Our goal is to offer comprehensive support for everyday tasks with real-time image-to-voice assistance.

How Sensory Steps Works

Real-Time Image-to-Voice Conversion

Automatically converts images of the surroundings into audio feedback, enhancing understanding of the environment.

Navigation Assistance

Provides real-time guidance for navigating various environments, ensuring a safe and seamless experience.

Object Recognition

Identifies and announces objects in the user's path, such as signs, doors, or obstacles.

Daily Task Support

Offers assistance for everyday tasks such as reading labels, identifying items, and navigating public spaces.
